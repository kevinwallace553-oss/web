Our story

Built for Sunday.
Built for ministry.

Bolt Kiosk started with a simple frustration: check-in software that was too expensive, too complicated, and not built for how churches actually work.

Why we built this

Check-In at a Breeze — it's more than a tagline.

Every Sunday morning, thousands of church volunteers struggle with check-in systems that were designed for corporate HR departments. Long setup processes. Per-seat fees that punish church growth. Features locked behind expensive tiers. Paper rosters printed at midnight Saturday.

We built Bolt Kiosk because we believed that a church with 50 members deserves the same quality tools as a church with 5,000 — and that "check-in software" should actually feel like a breeze to use.

"Your welcome team should be welcoming people, not looking up names."

Bolt Kiosk is a complete ministry management platform — six modules, one flat price, free to start. It works on any phone, tablet, or laptop. It works offline. It takes under 10 minutes to set up. And every feature we built, we built because a real ministry needed it.

What we believe

Our values

⚡
Speed is a feature
Every second your volunteer spends looking up a name is a second they're not looking at the person standing in front of them. We obsess over check-in speed.
🔒
Security is not optional
SHA-256 hashed passwords, server-side token validation, data isolation per organization, rate-limited logins. Your congregation's data is treated like it matters — because it does.
📖
Honest pricing always
No per-seat fees. No feature paywalls. No surprise charges when your church grows. One flat price, everything included. Growth should be celebrated, not penalized.
🤝
Built with churches
Every feature in Bolt Kiosk came from a real ministry need. Children's security tags, volunteer email RSVP, at-risk tracking — none of it was invented. All of it was asked for.
Where we're headed

The road ahead

Bolt Kiosk is currently available as a Progressive Web App — meaning it installs directly to your phone or tablet home screen with no app store required. You get offline support, push-to-home-screen install, and a full native app experience today.

We're actively working toward native iOS and Android apps via the App Store and Google Play, custom domain email (so invitations send from your church's own address), and multi-campus tools for growing organizations.

If you'd like to be notified when these launch, or if you have a feature request, reach out to us. Every church we talk to makes Bolt Kiosk better.
